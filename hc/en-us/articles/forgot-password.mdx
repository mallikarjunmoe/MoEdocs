# Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your MoEngage password, you can easily reset it using the password reset feature. This process is secure and will help you regain access to your account.

## Starting the Password Reset Process

### Step 1: Access the Login Page
1. Go to [MoEngage Login](https://dashboard.moengage.com/v4)
2. Click on "Forgot Password" link below the login form
3. You'll be taken to the password reset page

### Step 2: Enter Your Email
1. Enter the email address associated with your MoEngage account
2. Click "Send Reset Link"
3. You'll receive a confirmation message

### Step 3: Check Your Email
1. Check your email inbox for the password reset email
2. Look for an email from MoEngage
3. If you don't see it, check your spam/junk folder

## Password Reset Email

### Email Contents
The password reset email will contain:
- **Reset Link**: A secure link to reset your password
- **Expiration Time**: The link expires after a certain period
- **Security Information**: Details about the reset request
- **Contact Information**: How to get help if needed

### Using the Reset Link
1. Click the reset link in the email
2. You'll be taken to a secure password reset page
3. Enter your new password twice for confirmation
4. Click "Reset Password" to complete the process

## Creating a New Password

### Password Requirements
Your new password must meet these criteria:
- **Minimum Length**: At least 8 characters
- **Complexity**: Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- **Uniqueness**: Cannot be the same as your previous password
- **No Personal Info**: Avoid using personal information

### Strong Password Tips
- **Use a Passphrase**: Consider using a memorable phrase
- **Mix Characters**: Combine letters, numbers, and symbols
- **Avoid Common Words**: Don't use dictionary words
- **Make it Unique**: Don't reuse passwords from other accounts

## Troubleshooting Password Reset

### Common Issues

#### Email Not Received
- **Check Spam Folder**: Look in your spam/junk folder
- **Wait a Few Minutes**: Email delivery can take time
- **Check Email Address**: Ensure you entered the correct email
- **Contact Support**: If still not received, contact support

#### Reset Link Expired
- **Request New Link**: Start the reset process again
- **Check Email Quickly**: Reset links have limited validity
- **Use Recent Email**: Make sure you're using the latest reset email

#### Invalid Reset Link
- **Copy Link Correctly**: Ensure the entire link is copied
- **Check for Typos**: Verify the link is complete
- **Try Again**: Request a new reset link if needed

### Technical Issues
- **Browser Problems**: Try a different browser
- **Clear Cache**: Clear your browser cache and cookies
- **Disable Extensions**: Temporarily disable browser extensions
- **Check Internet**: Ensure you have a stable internet connection

## Security Considerations

### Account Security
- **Immediate Login**: Log in immediately after resetting
- **Check Account Activity**: Review recent account activity
- **Enable 2FA**: Consider enabling two-factor authentication
- **Update Other Devices**: Update password on all devices

### Preventing Future Issues
- **Password Manager**: Use a password manager to store passwords
- **Regular Updates**: Change passwords periodically
- **Secure Storage**: Don't write passwords down insecurely
- **Unique Passwords**: Use different passwords for different accounts

## After Password Reset

### First Login
1. Use your new password to log in
2. You may be prompted to update security settings
3. Review your account settings
4. Consider enabling additional security features

### Account Verification
- **Check Profile**: Verify your profile information is correct
- **Review Permissions**: Ensure your account permissions are appropriate
- **Update Security**: Review and update security settings
- **Test Features**: Make sure all features are working properly

## Alternative Recovery Methods

### Two-Factor Authentication
If you have 2FA enabled:
- **Use Recovery Codes**: Use your saved recovery codes
- **Contact Support**: Reach out to support for assistance
- **Verify Identity**: You may need to verify your identity

### Account Recovery
If you can't access your email:
- **Contact Support**: Reach out to MoEngage support
- **Verify Identity**: Provide proof of account ownership
- **Alternative Email**: Use an alternative email if available
