# Sign Up with MoEngage or Create a New Account

Creating a MoEngage account is the first step to start engaging with your customers effectively.

## Creating a New Account

### Step 1: Visit the Sign-Up Page
1. Go to [MoEngage Sign Up](https://www.moengage.com/signup)
2. Click on "Get Started" or "Sign Up"

### Step 2: Provide Basic Information
1. Enter your email address
2. Create a strong password
3. Provide your company name
4. Select your industry

### Step 3: Verify Your Email
1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. Complete the email verification process

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile
1. Add your name and contact information
2. Set up your workspace
3. Choose your data center region

## Account Types

### Free Trial
- 14-day free trial
- Access to core features
- Limited usage quotas

### Paid Plans
- Monthly or annual billing options
- Higher usage limits
- Advanced features and support
