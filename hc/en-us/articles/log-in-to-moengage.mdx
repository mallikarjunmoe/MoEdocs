# Log In to MoEngage

Accessing your MoEngage dashboard is simple and secure. Follow these steps to log in to your account.

## Logging In

### Step 1: Navigate to Login Page
1. Go to [MoEngage Login](https://dashboard.moengage.com/v4)
2. Or visit the main MoEngage website and click "Login"

### Step 2: Enter Credentials
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Step 3: Complete 2FA (if enabled)
1. If you have 2-step verification enabled, enter the code
2. Use your authenticator app or SMS code
3. Click "Verify" to complete login

## Login Options

### Remember Me
- Check "Remember me" to stay logged in
- Useful for trusted devices
- Session will persist for 30 days

### Forgot Password
- Click "Forgot Password" if you can't remember your password
- Enter your email address
- Follow the password reset instructions

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### Common Problems

#### Wrong Credentials
- Double-check your email and password
- Ensure Caps Lock is not enabled
- Try typing your password in a text editor first

#### Account Locked
- Too many failed login attempts
- Wait 15 minutes before trying again
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### 2FA Issues
- Check your authenticator app for the correct code
- Ensure your device time is synchronized
- Use recovery codes if available

### Browser Issues
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Disable browser extensions temporarily

## Security Best Practices

### Strong Passwords
- Use a unique, complex password
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid using personal information

### Secure Devices
- Only log in from trusted devices
- Log out when using shared computers
- Keep your browser updated

### Regular Security Checks
- Review your login activity regularly
- Update your password periodically
- Keep your 2FA settings current
